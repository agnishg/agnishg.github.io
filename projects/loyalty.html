Loyalty Programs

I started this program to reduce churn and increase customer wallet share This was initially piloted for the flagship product - Gold Loan, which has close to high contribution to profitability. Later, the program was adopted for other products as well.

KRAs :

  • Customer Surveys, Analysis
  • Communication Strategy
  • Stakeholder Management
  • Reward Tier Management
  • Budget Monitoring
  • Marketing Analytics
  • Rating:
  • Brands: Muthoot Fincorp, Muthoot EXIM, Muthoot Capital Services
  • Completed: July 2020

View More

In my last organisation, I launched a loyalty program to improve brand affinity and loyalty among our customers during Covid-19. The particular vertical Muthoot Exim, offers affordable precious metals in Jewellery and Coin form. I felt that this was a great opportunity to drive synergy, which would benefit the EXIM customers, and help drive growth. This was the brain-child of one of our directors. Therefore, I expressed my concerns, I finally decided to get on board and launch it. This led to a critical loss of time and resources, which could have been better utilised.

During the planning phase, I realised that 70% of the customer base had Gold Coins, which is not permitted for lending as per regulatory norms. Also, I suggested that if the objective is to drive loyalty, all customers, irrespective of the amount of Gold they bought, would be eligible for the offer. Otherwise, there was a chance of this being detrimental.

I had a chance to utilise my learnings in a subsequently loyalty project between another group company. When there was pushbacks, I mentioned the case, and we decided to pilot it first and then incorporate the learnings. The program is now in full swing, and the adoption rate is 30%, which is pretty great.
