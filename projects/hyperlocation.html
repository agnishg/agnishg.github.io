Hyperlocation

This was a project I handled to map the location of 3600+ branches pan India to improve customer discovery and ease effort required for visiting the retail branches

KRAs :

  • Data Analysis
  • Agency Evaluation and Onboarding
  • Local SEO
  • Google MyBusiness Integration
  • Brand Asset Management
  • Landing Page
  • Lead Flow
  • Rating:
  • Brand: Muthoot Fincorp
  • Website: https://branches.muthootfincorp.com/
  • Completed: August 2020

View More

The existing branch locator feature in the mobile app and website was textual, and the user experience cumbersome. From Google analytics report, we saw that while this was the 3rd most visited page, the bounce rates were much higher than our internal benchmarks.I was given the responsibility to implement the hyper location project across all branches within three months. The objective was to ease customer effort and improve experience while searching on the app and website.

My initial plan was to engage a vendor, who has had previous experience in such projects. I engaged the local operational teams to capture the accurate GPS locations, and sourced the balance information from our database. All the raw data was given to the vendor as per my project plan. A month into the project, on analysis of the first set of branches integrated, I noticed a lot of errors. As I tried to zero in on how to rectify the issue and solve the overarching problem, I also started researching on Local SEO and Google My Business features. I decided to include this integration to my current project, but with a limited scope of 200 branches. With the new offering in regional languages, I predicted increase in use based on the primary market statistics available. I took my CMO and the leadership team into confidence, and we decided to proceed with a specialised vendor - SingleInterface for this.

While the results were slow initially, the decision turned out to a huge advantage for us. For the 200 branches where this was piloted, we saw a 100% increase in lead acquisition and our revenues increased by 50% through this channel. During the Business listing, I found 70% of our branches were incorrectly mapped, which got corrected. Our bounce rate for the branch locator page reduced by 50%, and the listings helped improve Localised SEO, because of which we started ranking in the 2nd position for specific locations. The project was later scaled up to include all the branches.
