WhatsApp for Business

I worked on this project to Launch a new Communication channel for the flagship company of Muthoot Pappachan Group - Muthoot Fincorp, which serves 100k+ customers everyday through its 3600+ retail branches pan India.

KRAs :

  • Proposal Design with Use Cases and ROI for Approval
  • Project Management
  • Vendor Analysis and Selection
  • Communication Strategy
  • Lead Nurturing Flow
  • Creatives Design, Agency Liasing
  • Monitor Traffic & Conversion Metrics
  • Incorporate Learnings

During the onset of Covid-19 in India, our business was severely under the grind due to the nation-wide lockdown. This meant that our primary customer touchpoint, the retail branches remained closed until the lockdown was lifted. The most pressing challenge during this time was servicing the existing customers, answer their queries, because the call centres just could not handle the rush. Through analysis of customer conversations at the call centre and social media channels, feedback from sales teams, it became clear that 80% of the queries were pertaining to loan moratorium, missing payment due date and new loan enquiries.

To solve this problem, I proposed to launch a WhatsApp for Business service as an additional communication channel, as this is an app used by the majority of our customers, some of them even with feature phones. We planned for a phased wise approach, with a minimum viable product with faqs including Loan Moratorium, alternate payment channels, and product information in phase 1. For phase 2, I planned for account information and payments as integrations with our databases would take longer time. Apis would have to be designed and created, and we planned for them in phase -2. I placed strong emphasis on testing so that there are no issues post launch.

Through collaboration with IT, operations, legal and the service provider, I was able to launch phase 1 within one week, where we started seeing customers interaction reaching an average of 800-1200/ day, which was much higher than what we had expected. Post launch of phase-2, this metric nearly doubled and subsequently the call centre load reduced by 30%. This action further gave direction to our future digital marketing strategy, and WhatsApp became a platform where we 25% of all our digital transactions took place.
