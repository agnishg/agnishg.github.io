Will Digital Rule the World

The constant tug of war between proponents of Digital and Traditional channels should finally be settled, thanks to the Covid Pandemic. A lot of changed in the digital world since then.

Here are 3 data points for Marketers to consider for a future proof Marketing Strategy:

  1. Automated Customer Journeys
  2. Omni Channel Strategy
  3. Use of AI

1. Automated Customer Journeys

The marketing automation industry is growing and has become one of the most important trend in digital marketing. According to recent studies, marketing automation on average drives upto 14.5% increase in sales productivity and a 12.2% reduction in marketing overhead. 80% of marketing automation users saw an increase in the number of leads using marketing automation software, and 77% had an increase of conversions. 91% of marketing automation users agree it is “very important” to the overall success of their online marketing activities. Check out the infographic - The Rise of Marketing Automation” by Invespcro.

Companies have pivoted to use technology to automate the complete customer journey right from brand discovery to purchase to service. While more used in industries such as e-commerce, domains such as banking, with the advent of Fin-tech have made customer onboarding and service a truly 100% digital experience.

When personalisation-at-scale is done right, enterprises achieve a net 5.63% increase in sales revenue, 10.26% increase in order frequency, uplifts in average order value and an impressive 13.25% improvement in cross-sell/up-sell opportunities. The benefits transcend marketing alone and drive higher customer satisfaction metrics as well.

2. Omni Channel Strategy

Marketing automation works wonders with an Omni Channel Strategy. The 2020 Omni-channel Statistics Report reveals that marketers using at least three channels for campaigns enjoyed 287% more sales than those only targeting a single channel. Omni-channel marketing is essential for reaching today’s consumers.

A 2020 NielsenIQ report stated that COVID-19 fuelled a 50% increase in U.S. omni-channel purchases last year, but less than half of the activity led to online conversions. This essentially tells us that customers are using digital channels for product research. Seamless integration between online and offline experiences, including that between each touchpoint based on consumer needs and preferences is key to drive synergy across channels.

Continuous optimisation of attribution analysis is also important to understand effect of individual channels which will help CMOs make accurate budget decisions.

3. Use of AI

AI can be of tremendous business value when used correctly to analyse Big Data from Public and Internal Sources, implement Machine Learning to predict outcomes and create dynamic campaigns . Successful AI driven strategies can help implement hyper customised experiences and drive enterprise wide value such as loyalty and retention, which directly contribute to revenue growth.
